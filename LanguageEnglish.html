The Top 5 tallest Skyscrapers

Here are the Top 5 tallest Skyscrapers in the world

5. Lotte World Tower. The Lotte World Tower sits in Seoul, South Korea.It was completed in 2017.It is 1819 Feet tall and has 123 floors.This building has the biggest indoor Theme park in the world,but it also has other things like a outdoor park,a Theater,a Korean cultural museum and a shopping mall.

4. Makkah Royal Clock Tower. The Makkah Royal Clock Tower was completed in 2012. It is in Mecca,Saudi Arabia and has height of 1972 Feet and has 120 floors.The Makkah Royal Clock Tower is a mix residential apartments and hotel rooms. It consists of 120 floors.The building consists of 7 towers and in these 7 towers you can find a grand Mosque.There are 5 star Hotels, a shopping mall and resort accommodations for Hajj.

3. Shanghai Tower. The Shanghai Tower was built in Shanghai, China in 2015.It has 128 floors and has a height of 2073 Feet.They officially started building the tower in 1997,but the build stopped multiple times due to financial problems.The Shanghai Tower consists of 9 sections and insisde these sections are Cafes and gardens.

2. Tokyo Skytree . The Tokyo Skytree is the second tallest tower in the world with a height of 2080 feet. It was completed in Tokyo, Japan in 2012. it is one of the tallest free standing Towers in the World. The structure is resistant to Earthquakes.And the design is a mix of Modern and traditional Japenese design.

1. Burj Khalifa. The Burj Khalifa has been the tallest tower in the world for 13 years now with a height of 2717 Feet.It was built in Dubai, United Arab Emirates(UAE).The tower has 163 floors.Inside they have lots of Luxury apartments ,gourmet resturants, High-End Hotels and company suites.

Honorable Mention. One World Trade Center. One World Trade Center was built in 2014. It is located in Manhattan,NYC,US. It has 94 floors. The Trade Center is 1776 feet long. It is also known as the Freedom Tower. It is the tallest building in the Western Hemisphere.

The Top 5 biggest mountains

Here are the Top 5 biggest mountains in the world

5. Mt Makalu. Mt Makalu is the 5th tallest mountain in the world. Its height is 27,825 feet or 8,481 m. It is on the border between China and Nepal. It was first climbed by an American team in 1954.

4. Mt Lhotse. Mt. Lhotse is the 4th tallest mountain in the world with a height of 27,940 feet/8,516 m. It also lies on the border between Nepal and Tibet(China). It was first climbed in May, 1956 by Mr. Fritz Luchsinger and Ernst Reiss. Mt. Lhotse is now rarely climbed because it is one of the most difficult and dangerous mountains.

3. Mount Kangchenjunga. Mount Kangchenjunga is the 3rd tallest mountain in the world and is located on the border between India and Nepal. It is 28,169 (8,586 m) tall. Kangchenjunga is the largest mountain in the east side of the world.

2. K2. K2 is the 2nd tallest mountain in the world after Mount Everest. It is located in Pakistan in the Karakoram Range. It measures 28,251 feet or 8,611 m. The K2 mountain is also one of the most dangerous mountains in the world, very few people have attempted to climb the mountain and a few have died. The first most successful team was an Italian expedition team that climbed the mountain on July 31, 1954.

1. Mount Everest. Mount Everest is the tallest mountain in the world. It is 29,029 feet (8,848 m) tall and is located in Nepal near the Nepal-China border. Mount Everest is much easier to climb than eg K2 . Mount Everest was first scaled in 1953 by a New Zealand mountaineer and a British expedition team. It is a goal for many people to climb the mountain.
